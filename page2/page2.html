Hooks and Humor: Crochet as a Social Activity

Crochet has always been a social craft, but in the year 2000, it took on a new dimension.

Crochet circles and clubs became popular, offering a space for enthusiasts to gather, share tips, and, most importantly, laugh. These gatherings were about more than just crochet; they were a chance to build friendships and community.

The rise of crochet-themed events and conventions further cemented its status as a social activity.
